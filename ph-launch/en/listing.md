## Description (250–500 words)

```
Most podcasters spend 5–8 hours editing every episode.
That's not podcasting — that's a second job.

PodAha is an AI-powered podcast production platform that handles everything
after you hit "record":

🎙️ AI Noise Reduction & Filler Word Removal
  Automatically cleans up "um", "uh", and background noise.
  No manual scrubbing through audio.

📝 Auto-Transcription & Show Notes
  95%+ accuracy transcription with timestamps.
  Generates episode titles, descriptions, and SEO-optimized show notes.

✂️ Golden Quote Extraction
  AI finds your best 30–90 second clips for social media.
  Turn every episode into 5–8 short-form videos automatically.

🚀 One-Click Publishing
  Publish directly to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and RSS.
  No copy-pasting between apps.

---

Who is PodAha for?

→ Solo podcasters who want to publish consistently without burning out
→ Content creators repurposing audio into multi-format content
→ Indie developers building thought leadership through podcasting
→ Coaches and educators turning their expertise into audio products

The result: what used to take 5–8 hours now takes under 1 hour.

---

Pricing:
→ Free: 300 credits (~5 hours of audio)
→ Plus: $9.90/month (1,000 credits, ~17 hours)
→ Pro: $19.90/month (2,500 credits, ~40 hours)

We started because we were tired of spending more time editing than creating.
PodAha is the tool we wished existed.

Try it free at podaha.com — no credit card required.
```
