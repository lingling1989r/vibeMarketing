# Hunter Outreach Templates

> Send these 10-14 days before launch.
> Target: Hunters with 500+ followers who cover AI/productivity/creator tools.
> Send via Twitter DM or email. Personalize [brackets].

---

## Finding Good Hunters

Search on Product Hunt:
- Go to https://www.producthunt.com/hunters
- Filter by: followers (500+), recent activity (last 3 months)
- Look for hunters who've hunted AI / productivity / content tools

Good hunter profile checklist:
- [ ] 500+ followers on PH
- [ ] Active in last 60 days (commented, upvoted)
- [ ] Has hunted products similar to yours (AI, creator tools, SaaS)
- [ ] Responsive on Twitter or email
